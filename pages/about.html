My Journey

I'm Mark Yang, an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Northeastern University with a deep passion for both technology and the outdoors. My journey into engineering began with a curiosity about how things work, which naturally evolved into building things that solve real problems.

What sets me apart is my unique perspective that comes from rock climbing. The same methodical approach I use to solve a challenging boulder problem - breaking it down into sequences, finding the most efficient path, and persisting through failure - is exactly how I approach engineering challenges.
